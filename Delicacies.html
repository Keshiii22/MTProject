Delicacies

Batac Empanada

The Batac empanada is a deep-fried, orange-colored pastry from Batac, Ilocos Norte, Philippines, characterized by its crunchy rice flour wrapper and savory filling of grated green papaya, munggo beans, a whole egg, and pieces of local longganisa. This unique Ilocano street food is a local delicacy and a popular tourist snack, often served with sukang Iloko (Ilocano vinegar)

Batac Longganisa

Batac longganisa is a flavorful Filipino pork sausage from Batac, Ilocos Norte, known for its salty, tangy, and strongly garlicky taste. It's a staple ingredient in the region's iconic Batac empanada and is often made without preservatives and with the use of Sukang Iloko (Ilocos vinegar)

Batac Bagnet

Batac bagnet is a deep-fried pork belly dish from Ilocos, Philippines, prepared by first boiling and air-drying a slab of pork belly before deep-frying it until the skin is exceptionally crispy and the meat remains tender. It is a local delicacy known for its crunchy texture and savory flavor, often seasoned with garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves during cooking.

Batac Miki

Batac Miki, or Miki ti Batac, is an Ilocano-style noodle soup featuring fresh miki noodles in a flavorful chicken broth, distinguished by its yellow-orange color from annatto powder or seeds. It is a popular, comforting dish from Batac, Ilocos Norte, typically served with toppings like boiled eggs, shredded chicken, and ground crispy pork (bagnet) or chicharon, and garnished with scallions and fried garlic.

Batac Pakbet

Batac pakbet is a variation of the traditional Filipino dish pinakbet, from the Ilocos region, that features a medley of local vegetables stewed in a savory shrimp paste (bagoong alamang) or fish paste (bagoong isda) sauce. The dish's name comes from the Ilocano word "pinakebbet," meaning "shriveled," which refers to the cooked vegetables. Batac pinakbet often includes ingredients like bitter melon (ampalaya), eggplant, okra, and string beans, and is typically enhanced with pork belly (bagnet) for added flavor.
