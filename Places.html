Places to Go

Marcos Museum

The Marcos Museum and Mausoleum in Batac, Ilocos Norte, is a memorial to former Philippine President Ferdinand E. Marcos, showcasing his memorabilia, political career, and life in his ancestral home. It houses exhibits detailing his military service, political journey, and the history of the Marcos family, including Imelda Marcos's belongings and historical photographs. The site also includes a mausoleum where his embalmed body was displayed in a glass coffin before its re-interment in 2016; a wax replica is now shown in the glass coffin.

Ricarte Park

Ricarte Park in Batac is the General Artemio Ricarte National Shrine, a historical park with a museum and library honoring General Artemio Ricarte, a key figure in the Philippine Revolution and the "Father of the Philippine Army". The park features a monument to Ricarte, a museum with his memorabilia, a library, and lush green landscapes perfect for peaceful walks and picnics.

Batac Church

The Batac Church, or the Immaculate Conception Parish Church, is a historic Roman Catholic church in Batac, Ilocos Norte, built in the 17th century. The present structure is a 1692 reconstruction that features Baroque-style architecture, characterized by its palitada (decorative plaster), large rose window, and heavy pilasters. The church serves as a significant historical and cultural landmark, reflecting the town's Spanish colonial past and remaining a center of community faith.

Batac Market

The Batac market is a large regional marketplace offering fresh local produce, native delicacies like longganisa and empanada, handicrafts, and other goods. Adjacent to the public market is the Delicia Center, containing various shops and services, while a new agricultural trading center is being developed nearby to support local farmers. The market is a significant commercial and cultural hub, featuring the famous Batac Empanada and hosting events like the Farmers Festival and Empanada Festival.

Batac Riverside

The riverside in Batac, known as the Batac Riverside Empanadaan, is a bustling food hub and tourist spot famous for serving the authentic Ilocos empanada and other local delicacies like longganisa and miki soup. This vibrant destination features numerous food stalls offering affordable, fresh, and delicious street food, a lively atmosphere for locals and tourists, and a place to buy souvenirs.
